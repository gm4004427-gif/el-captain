1️⃣ كتابة الفقرة الإنشائية (Writing a Paragraph)

عندما يُطلب منك كتابة ستة سطور عن موضوع معين، فهذا يعني كتابة فقرة إنشائية (Paragraph). تتكون الفقرة من 3 أجزاء رئيسية:

  • 1. Topic sentence (جملة الموضوع): وهي الجملة الأولى التي تحمل الفكرة الرئيسية للموضوع.
  • 2. Supporting sentences (جمل التفاصيل الداعمة): وهي جمل تناقش تفاصيل الموضوع وتعطي أمثلة.
  • 3. Conclusion / Closing sentence (الجملة الختامية): وهي جملة تلخص ما تم مناقشته في الفقرة، وتبدأ بتعبيرات مثل (To sum up, In conclusion, Finally).

💡 خطوات هامة عند الكتابة:

  • 1. اترك مسافة حوالي (1 سم) في بداية السطر الأول للفقرة (Indent).
  • 2. ابدأ بالجملة الرئيسية التي تدل على الموضوع (يُفضل أن تكون في صيغة سؤال لجذب الانتباه).
  • 3. استخدم جملاً قصيرة وبسيطة للكتابة عن تفاصيل الفكرة.
  • 4. لا تنس أن تبدأ كل جملة بحرف (Capital) وتنهيها بنقطة (.) أو علامة استفهام (?) حسب الحاجة.
  • 5. اختتم فقرتك بجملة عامة تلخص الموضوع.
  • 6. راجع فقرتك بحثاً عن أخطاء القواعد (Grammar).
  • 7. راجع مرة أخرى بحثاً عن أخطاء الكتابة والتهجي (Spelling).
  • 8. راجع مرة أخيرة للتأكد من دقة علامات الترقيم (Punctuation).
  • 9. انقل موضوعك إلى المكان المخصص في ورقة الإجابة مع التأكد من الحجم المطلوب.

🌟 نماذج للفقرات الإنشائية (Model Paragraphs)

1. How can you develop yourself to meet the challenges of the future
Are you ready for the future? You need to think carefully before you reply to this question. Getting a university degree does not mean you are ready for the future. To be ready to face the challenges of the future, you need to be a lifelong learner. The world is changing quickly and this will continue. To make yourself ready for whatever challenges you may meet, never stop learning and acquiring new skills. To conclude, go on learning as long as you are alive.
2. Your duty towards your country
Do you know what your duties towards your country are? We all live safely in our country. It is our duty to do our best to make our country one of the most developed countries in the world. Our country had a great past. We need to do all that to make it have a better present and the best future. If everyone in their field works hard, our country will develop and prosper. To sum up, we all have to be soldiers that fight in all fields of life for our country.
3. Honesty is an outstanding quality
Have you ever thought about how important honesty is? Honesty is a valuable human value. It is great to be honest. It is greater if you have honest people around. Our society is badly in need of honest doctors, engineers, teachers, traders, workers...etc. If a person is honest, he or she is expected to do their work well. If a trader is honest, he or she does not cheat in the quality or the price of goods. To sum up, honesty is a quality we cannot do without.
4. Ways to help others rather than offering money
Is money the only thing one can help others with? Absolutely not! One can help others in several ways. When you work for a charity, you help others. If you donate blood, you help others. You can help others by keeping the streets clean, not making noise, using public property wisely,...etc. A good neighbour helps others by being good. A doctor can help others by doing their job in the best way possible. In short, money is not the only way to help others.

2️⃣ فقرة المقدمة للمقال (Introductory Paragraph)

لكتابة مقدمة جذابة للمقال (Essay)، اتبع الآتي:

  • ابدأ بجملة لجذب الانتباه تُسمى Hook أو Attention grabber (ويُفضل أن تكون على هيئة سؤال).
  • اكتب جملة أو جملتين لطرح الموضوع الذي يدور حوله المقال وتسمى Thesis.
  • أعطِ خلفية بسيطة عن الموضوع Background.
  • اذكر الطريقة التي ستتناول بها الموضوع.
Model: How to achieve success (Intro)
Do you know how to achieve true success? (Hook) True success is when you manage to achieve all or most of your goals. (Thesis) Achieving success makes you feel happy and trustful. In this essay, I am going to write about how to achieve success in both personal life and work life. I am going to depend on my personal experience, but sometimes I am going to refer to other experts' opinions. Let's start by throwing light on how to achieve personal success.

3️⃣ فقرة الخاتمة للمقال (Conclusion Paragraph)

لكتابة خاتمة قوية تلخص المقال اتبع الآتي:

  • ابدأ بأحد العبارات الختامية مثل:
    In conclusion / Finally / To sum up / To conclude / To put the whole matter in a nutshell.
  • أعد صياغة الفكرة الرئيسية (Restate the main idea).
  • اذكر رأيك الشخصي (Your personal opinion).
  • اختتم الفقرة بطريقة شيقة تترك أثراً.
Model: How to achieve success (Conclusion)
In conclusion, we all look forward to achieving success in both our personal life and work life. Achieving success is not easy, but it is not impossible either. If you want to succeed, first you need to have a clear goal. This goal has to be appropriate to your abilities. Achieving a goal needs good planning, time management and hard work. You also need to be intelligent and flexible. Once you reach your goal, you will feel true happiness and satisfaction.
